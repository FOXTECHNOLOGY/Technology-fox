Welcome to MM HIGH SCHOOL - KAMULI

At MM High School, we nurture academic excellence, sportsmanship, and holistic development. Our school has won 4 trophies in one term across different games, and we aim to continue excelling in both academics and sports.

School Background

MM High School was founded with the aim of providing quality education and a conducive learning environment. Our curriculum is designed to help students excel in national and international examinations.

We are proud of our sports achievements. Our students participate in various games including football, basketball, athletics, and volleyball. In one term, we won 4 trophies in different competitions!
